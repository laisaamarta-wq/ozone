A contraindications checklist is completed at intake. Clients with hyperthyroidism, pregnancy, or active haemorrhage are advised against therapy.

Most clients notice improved energy and reduced muscle soreness within hours of their first session. Sleep improvements typically appear after 2–3 sessions. Anti-inflammatory effects accumulate over the course of a protocol — significant changes in joint pain and chronic inflammation are typically observed after 4–6 sessions.

For maintenance and general wellbeing: once per week. For active recovery (training, post-surgery): 2–3 times per week. For acute inflammation or targeted therapeutic protocols: daily sessions for short bursts of 5–10 days. Your practitioner will recommend the cadence that fits your specific goals.
